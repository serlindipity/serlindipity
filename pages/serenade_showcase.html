You're Still
the One

I dedicate this song cover for my parents. My parents have recently celebrated their 25th Wedding Anniversary last May. I look up to both of them, and being their daughter is one of my greatest blessings in life. One day I want to have the same relationship that they have. Just like the lyrics says 'You're still the one I want for life', I want my future partner to love me like how my dad continues to love my mom throughout the years of being together.

1/3

Say You
Won't Let Go

This song has always been my favorite. The bridge's lyrics in particular 'I wanna live with you, even when we're ghosts, 'cause you were always there for me when i needed you most'. With these lyrics, I realized that life is uncertain, you'll never know when or if you are going to lose your loved ones, especially since the pandemic happened. I realized that, it's really important to be there & spend time with the people you love while they're still here, and not take them for granted.

2/3

The One That
Got Away

Urban dictionary says that 'The One That Got Away' pertains to the person who you originally want to end up with, but due to a cause of fate or by consequences, the relationship failed, and as time goes by you wonder what you and that person could have been, making them the one that got away. If I would dedicate this song, I would dedicate it to my loved ones that I've lost. Continuing to live life without them is hard & makes me wonder, maybe in another lifetime or timeline, we'll be reunited ..and there, we'll be able to keep our promises.

3/3
